Who is Nicholas?

Nicholas Armstrong is a technology enthusiast living in Waterloo, Ontario. Currently Head of Software Development, North America at Carnegie Technologies, I supervise the development of Carnegie’s Network Convergence Platform™. Our platform offers truly ubiquitous, always-on connectivity between Wi-Fi and cellular networks, for voice and video with no gaps and no dropped sessions, and is available for both mobile devices and connected vehicles. In this capacity, I have traveled throughout Asia, Europe, and North America to interact with operators, OEMs, and other technology companies.

Prior to Carnegie, I received a Master’s of Applied Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Waterloo and held positions with Pravala Networks, Microsoft, and more.

My development interests include user experience, interface design, high performance web applications, and web services. My recent work has focused on delivering high-performance web services to drive mobile clients and web applications at scale, and single-page web applications built on top of those services. Over the course of my career I have worked extensively with node.js, Android, WPF, SQL, and .NET, and have experience in numerous other languages, platforms, tools, and environments.
