Who is Nicholas?

Nicholas Armstrong is a technology enthusiast currently living in Waterloo, Ontario. A graduate of Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo (BASc and MASc), I work full-time at Pravala Networks, a startup focused on improving multi-network experiences on mobile devices through Wi-Fi offload, bandwidth aggregation, quality monitoring, and network policy. As VP Engineering, I manage development of Pravala's policy-based network platform, oversee customer deployments, and work closely with our partners and customers. In this capacity, I have traveled throughout Asia and North America to interact with operators, OEMs, and other technology companies.

My development interests include user experience, interface design, high performance web applications, and web services. My recent work has focused on delivering high-performance web services to drive mobile clients and web applications at scale, and single-page web applications built on top of those services. Over the course of my career I have worked extensively with node.js, Android, WPF, SQL, and .NET, and have experience in numerous other languages, platforms, tools, and environments.
